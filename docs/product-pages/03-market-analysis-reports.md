## The eleven-dimension framework

### Market Overview — six dimensions

1. **Retail Snapshot** — major retailers, channel mix (modern / traditional / e-com), price-band segmentation, private-label strength
2. **Market Size & Demand** — TAM, historical trends, current-year estimates, 3–5 year demand outlook
3. **Consumer Preferences** — preferred formats, packaging, quality tiers, brand loyalty vs. price sensitivity, cultural drivers
4. **Market Sentiment** — trade press, buyer feedback, social signals, regulatory climate
5. **Seasonal Demand** — month-by-month profile, peaks, troughs, regulatory seasonal triggers
6. **Local Production** — domestic supply capacity vs. structural import gap

### Trade Readiness — five dimensions

7. **Substitutes & Competitors** — direct substitutes, price positioning, market share estimates
8. **SWOT Analysis** — product-country specific strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
9. **Entry Barriers** — licensing, phytosanitary and food safety standards, distribution complexity, buyer concentration
10. **Packaging & Labelling** — mandatory language, nutrition labels, origin marking, sustainability mandates
11. **Quality & Certifications** — required certs (HACCP, BRC, IFS, SQF, ISO, EN, ASTM), timelines and cost estimates
