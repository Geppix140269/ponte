## MR-006 — US Trade Intelligence Report ✦ NEW

**Pricing TBD  ·  Delivery TBD  ·  New product**

The decision: *"Specifically for the US — who's importing what, from
where, and at what price?"*

A US-focused trade-intelligence report combining official US Census
Bureau Foreign Trade statistics with ICTTM transaction-level customs and
bill-of-lading data. The Census layer gives authoritative volume and
value totals; the transaction layer adds company-level granularity —
which US companies are importing the product, and from which foreign
suppliers.

### You configure

- HS code (one)
- Trade direction: imports / exports / both
- Time period (default: last 12 months)

### What you get in the PDF

- US import and export volumes and values — monthly trend, MoM, YoY
- Top origin/destination countries ranked by volume and value
- **Top US importing companies and top foreign suppliers** (transaction-level)
- Port-level data: leading US ports of entry by volume
- Price analysis: average unit value vs. global benchmark
- **Section 301 tariff impact analysis** where applicable
- Trade balance: US net position, 5-year trend
- Analyst commentary and outlook
- Methodology appendix and full source citations
- Single-organisation licence

### Why this report is data-intensive

The US is one of very few markets where both **official statistics**
(Census Bureau) AND **transaction-level customs / BoL data** are
publicly available. MR-006 layers both: Census for authoritative totals
plus transaction data for company-level granularity. No other country
allows that depth from public sources.

### When to commission MR-006

- You're a foreign exporter targeting the US market
- You're a US importer benchmarking sourcing costs against competitors
- You're a US manufacturer assessing import competition
- You're a trade attorney working on a Section 301 case
- You're a government body monitoring US market access

### MR-006 vs. MR-001

MR-006 is US-specific and data-intensive — it answers *"who is trading
this product in the US and at what volumes and prices?"* MR-001 is the
deeper analyst engagement covering market-entry strategy, competitive
landscape, barriers, and go-to-market — for ANY country including the
US. A buyer who reads MR-006 and wants the full US market-entry strategy
commissions MR-001 next.

## SF-001 — EU Seafood Trade Report ✦ NEW

**Pricing TBD  ·  Delivery TBD  ·  Specialist regional report**

The decision: *"How is this seafood species moving into the EU this
week, at what price, from which origin?"*

A specialist trade-intelligence report for the EU seafood sector,
anchored in **EU Taxud weekly customs surveillance data** — the most
granular and timely official trade dataset available for any sector
globally.

### Why this report is unique

EU Taxud tracks EU seafood imports at **weekly frequency**, broken down
by species, country of origin, and EU member state of import. Most
official trade statistics are monthly or quarterly and published with a
2–3 month lag. Taxud data is typically available **within days of the
reference week**.

For seafood markets where prices move weekly, that frequency changes
the entire game. There is no global or single-country structured product
that delivers what weekly Taxud delivers for EU seafood — which is why
SF-001 is the lone regional exception to Ponte's "global OR one country"
rule.

### You configure

- Seafood species or HS code within Chapter 03, 1604, or 1605
- Reference week
- Comparison period: prior week / same week prior year / YTD vs prior year

### What you get in the PDF

- Weekly import volumes and values by species and EU member state
- Top origin countries by species: market share, trend, emerging suppliers
- Average landed price per kg by species and origin — weekly movements
  and seasonal pattern
- Year-over-year and week-on-week volume and price comparison
- Tariff and TRQ (Tariff Rate Quota) utilisation rates by species and origin
- Supply-chain alerts: volume spikes, price anomalies, new entrants
- Senior-analyst commentary
- Methodology appendix and full source citations
- Single-organisation licence

### When to commission SF-001

- You're an **EU seafood importer** monitoring competitor sourcing
- You're an **exporter to the EU** tracking landed-price benchmarks
- You're a **seafood trader** managing price risk on weekly cycles
- You're a **bank underwriting seafood trade finance** and need recent
  market data, not 3-month-old statistics
- You're an **industry association** monitoring sector health
